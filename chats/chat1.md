8. Competitive landscape & differentiation
No one has built for the full longitudinal arc of the fertility experience. Every existing player serves a specific slice.
Conceive is a D2C subscription app that recently added clinic-distributed patient software through an RMA-NY partnership announced in January 2025. It's focused on the active treatment window and hasn't been built for the full longitudinal arc.
Frame Fertility offers human coaching before and around a clinic consult. It's a strong pre-consult experience but stops at the clinic door and isn't longitudinal.
Berry Fertility is a D2C app distributed through a clinical partnership with Pinnacle (announced 2024). The product is distribution-dependent and serves a narrow patient wedge rather than the full arc.
Salve, now operating under Automate Health, builds clinic-side communications software. It's clinic-built and clinic-first, not patient-first in its design philosophy.
Alife Health is building AI for embryology and clinical decision support. It's clinical tooling for providers, not a patient experience platform.
Flo and Natural Cycles are cycle tracking apps used before a woman enters clinical treatment. They stop before treatment begins.
Progyny, Carrot, and Maven are employer fertility benefits providers — administration, navigation, and provider networks. They don't touch the in-clinic experience or longitudinal patient support.
Maven Clinic is a broad women's health platform serving everything from preconception through postpartum. It isn't fertility-specific and doesn't own the full fertility arc.
Carousel's differentiation:

Built across the full arc. No other player serves the 6-12 months before treatment, the daily experience during a cycle, and the 5-7 years of storage that follow.
Patient-first by design. Starts from patient research, not clinic operations or clinician workflows.
Personalized to the individual's profile, decisions, and stage — not a generic content library.
Founder-market fit. Founded by a 39-year-old who has been through three cycles herself and is actively inside the category.
Grounded in rigorous research — 28 longitudinal patient interviews before writing a line of product code.
