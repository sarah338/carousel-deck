Business model

One product. The woman’s experience is the same regardless of who pays.

Now

Clinic

The clinic adopts Carousel because reducing dropout protects cycle revenue. We sell the ROI, not the software.

Revenue-share or white-label, where the clinic gains a line of business — is upside we’ll model with pilot data.

Why first

It’s where the money problem is. The wedge that makes everything else credible.

Later

Employer

Maven and Carrot proved employers pay for fertility benefits. That lane is occupied and the sales motion is 12+ months out.

Why later

Validated demand, wrong time. We need the clinic proof point first.

Latest

Health Plan · System · Medicaid

Real at scale. Year-plus motion. Requires everything above to be true first.

Why latest

Largest pool, slowest buyer. Built for after we’ve earned the right to be in the room.
